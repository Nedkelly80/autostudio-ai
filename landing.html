How it works
Three steps, zero setup
1
Upload your car photo
Drop any JPG, PNG, or WEBP photo of a car — a race car, show car, daily driver, or render. The app accepts up to 10 MB and works with any angle or lighting condition.
Drag & drop · File picker · Up to 10 MB
2
Choose your tool and generate
Switch between Color Changer, Livery Generator, or Logo Placement. Pick a colour swatch or type a hex code, describe a livery in plain language, or upload a transparent PNG logo and position it on the interactive canvas.
SDXL ControlNet · Fal.ai inference · 4–8 seconds
3
Compare, refine, and export
Every result lands in the output gallery. Use the before/after comparison slider to evaluate changes, load any result back to the canvas for further iterations, then export as JPG or print a full PDF spec sheet.
Gallery · Compare slider · JPG export · PDF spec sheet
